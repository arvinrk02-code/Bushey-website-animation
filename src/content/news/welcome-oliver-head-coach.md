We are very pleased to tell you that we have a new Head Coach, Oliver Spisak.

Oliver will be attending the Herts Aquatic Open Meet with us, tomorrow, and then easing himself in over the next few weeks, as he hands over his duties at his old club.

Oliver has been a coach for almost ten years, with Ealing SC and Brent Dolphins, and some of you will recognise him from his guest appearance as surprise coach of our Competitive Squad a few weeks ago! He will be responsible for managing training and working with us all to make Bushey an even more successful and fun place to be, as we head into the big Centenary year.

It's an exciting time to be a member of this club, and we hope that you are as pleased as we are to welcome Oliver into the Bushey family. He is extremely enthusiastic, and eager to meet as many Bushey swimmers, coaches and parents as possible in the coming weeks.

In the meantime, Happy Halloween everyone!

Best wishes

The Committee
