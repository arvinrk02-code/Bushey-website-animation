Challenge is where swimming becomes a sport you train for, not just a skill you have. With up to five sessions a week across four pools, including invitation-only long-course training at Luton, swimmers build the engine that county-level racing demands.

Coaches begin tailoring training towards each swimmer's strongest events, and Hertfordshire county qualifying times become a realistic, named target for the season.
