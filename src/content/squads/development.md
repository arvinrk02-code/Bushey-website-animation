Development is the bridge between learning to swim and training to race. The Sunday session extends to 90 minutes and swimmers begin to build a genuine aerobic base, learning to hold technique together when they're tired, the single most important skill in competitive swimming.

Swimmers at this level are encouraged to enter Club Championships and, when ready, their first external galas with the club team.
