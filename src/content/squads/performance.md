Performance is the summit of the Bushey pathway. Swimmers here train up to five times a week on a fully periodised programme: aerobic blocks, race-pace phases, and a proper taper into the meets that matter.

Just as importantly, Performance swimmers are the role models of the club, leading warm-ups, supporting younger squads at galas, and showing what a hundred years of Bushey swimming looks like in the water.
