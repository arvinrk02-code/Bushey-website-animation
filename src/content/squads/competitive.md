Competitive squad swimmers plan a season, not just a session. Working with their coach they pick target meets, build towards taper, and learn how the pieces fit together: training blocks, race practice, recovery and the habits covered in our Athlete Hub.

Swimmers at this level regularly represent BASC at county qualifiers and open meets around the region, with Hertfordshire county finals the season's benchmark and East Region times the stretch goal.
