Improvement swimmers train with intent. Sessions introduce the pace clock properly, leaving on the top, holding target times, understanding what "descending" means, and racing becomes a regular, expected part of club life rather than an occasional event.

From age 9 we expect swimmers to represent BASC in league galas when selected. It's fun, it's loud, and it's where team spirit is made.
