Our Masters section is for adult swimmers, returning competitors, fitness swimmers, triathletes and parents who fancy getting back in the water. We're actively growing this part of the club during our centenary year.

Whether you want to race at Masters meets or simply train well once a week, get in touch and we'll find you a lane.
