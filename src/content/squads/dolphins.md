Dolphins is the first rung of the Bushey pathway. The emphasis is entirely on technique: our teachers care far more about a beautiful, efficient stroke than how fast it goes. Swimmers work in small groups with plenty of individual feedback, learning all four strokes plus the underwater skills, streamlining, sculling, push-and-glides . That everything else is built on.

There is no pressure to compete at this stage, but Dolphins swimmers are welcome at our internal fun galas, where racing is about taking part and getting a first taste of the big-event buzz.
