Sharks swimmers train twice a week and start to learn what being part of a squad means: following a set from the whiteboard, swimming with a lane etiquette, and supporting teammates. All four strokes are refined towards fully legal race technique, and dives and turns become a regular part of every session.

Sharks is where most swimmers experience their first club time trial, a friendly, low-pressure way to get an official time on the board and something to beat next term.
